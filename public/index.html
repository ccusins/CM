Other Sports

UK bookmakers set their odds based on the flow of money to a specific outcome among other factors.

For example if Arsenal were playing Tottenham and there is a sudden increase in the amount of bets placed for Arsenal to win due to news of an injury to a Tottenham player, bookmakers will lower the odds for Arsenal accordingly.

However bookmakers are quick to stop 'smart' gamblers. Therefore, the amount of money being placed based on this change of information will be lower and odds will be slower to move.

Hence we have scripts that leverage this slow movement of odds to find us these opportunities.
