While accounts can be created in any order, it is advisable to start with those with lower deposit requirements.

Deposits

For the initial batch of sign-ups, the required funds will be provided through a bank transfer, and deposit requests will be put in your 'deposits' section.

Subsequently, due to the nature of the betting, a portion of our funds may remain in your accounts, as indicated in the net balance section below. This surplus amount will be withdrawn to your account for future use in deposits.

If the net balance appears negative, additional funds will once again be provided for future deposits.

Please note that in order to profit from your accounts, deposits must be made through debit cards, not Paypal or any other payment processor.
